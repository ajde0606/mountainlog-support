Document every climb, tour, and summit with clarity.

Mountain Notebook is your secure alpine logbook—capture routes, conditions, avalanche observations, photos, and gear notes, then sync them across devices. Designed for mountaineers who want a reliable memory of every day in the peaks.

Download on the App Store

Why Mountain Notebook?

  • Record climbs, ski tours, and hut approaches with GPS context.
  • Attach avalanche observations and weather notes for each outing.
  • Secure storage with privacy-first local data and exports.
  • Photo and audio support to remember the details that matter.

Offline-friendly

Save entries where connectivity is limited, then sync when you are back in range.

Avalanche & Weather Notes

Log snowpack stability, wind, and visibility so you can reference past conditions before heading out.

Media-rich Journal

Add photos, audio clips, and gear lists alongside each route to keep your stories vivid.

Secure & Private

Your journal data stays yours. Export entries anytime and manage storage safely.

Privacy

Mountain Notebook is built for privacy-first record keeping. We minimize data collection and give you control over your information.

Data We Collect

Only the information you add in the app: journal entries, media attachments, and optional location notes used to contextualize your outings.

How It’s Used

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality such as syncing entries, displaying maps, and enabling exports. We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

Your Control

Export or delete your entries anytime. For privacy questions or data requests, contact hornliquant@gmail.com.

為什麼選擇 Mountain Notebook？

  • 離線記錄登山或滑雪行程，安全保存軌跡與照片
  • 標註坡向、坡度與雪層觀察，追蹤雪況變化
  • 整理路線難度、海拔、時間與備註，掌握行程摘要

智慧行程摘要

自動彙整日期、地點、坡度與海拔，讓每一次紀錄都清晰易讀。

相片與地圖

將照片與 GPS 路線一同保存，方便回顧地形與環境條件。

滑雪與雪況筆記

安全與私密

資料存於本機並可備份，沒有多餘追蹤或廣告。
